    About us

    We are a consulting firm dedicated to crypto assets projects. We have two branches: meta experience and project investements. We focus on investments that we believe have multiple aspects indicative of the potential for long-term success. We are a multidisciplinary team of engineers and finance specialists based in Lugano, Switzerland.
